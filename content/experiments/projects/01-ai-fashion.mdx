This project focuses on the application of generative AI models to explore new clothing silhouettes and beauty trends by generating new content - from images to video. During my time as an AI Intern at DE-YAN, I experimented extensively with training small fine-tuned datasets and, individually, developed a specific methodology for generating new content by testing various text-to-image and image-to-video architectures. My recent work involves replicative generation of luxury brand’s clothing pieces, which are then mapped onto real models using custom GenAI pipelines.
Further experiments involve the segmentation of archival pieces from established brands to analyze how AI models replicate and iterate on specific design languages. By utilizing image-to-image techniques, I test the limits of algorithmic creativity in simulating textiles, draping, and trend-driven aesthetics.
