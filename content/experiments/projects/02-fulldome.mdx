This project category documents my ongoing engagement with the unique architecture and immersive potential of the fulldome format. Beyond my 2024 project SPELL, I continue to experiment with how visual content can be mapped to embrace the specific curvature and scale of a dome. These experiments focus on the technical challenges of spatial distortion and 360-degree orientation, treating the dome not just as a screen, but as a site-specific environment for digital ritual and collective experience.
I performed a live video set during the IMMER festival, utilizing real-time visual processing to interact with a 30-foot dome infrastructure. My work in this format remains a core part of my practice as I continue to explore the technical shapes and narrative possibilities that make this display medium unique.
