This section showcase a portion of my learning and explorations into physical production through introductory and advanced fabrication courses during my Master’s degree. Having started with no prior knowledge of physical shop work, I utilized these two years to build a technical foundation in material handling and project assembly. I now apply these core skills in my professional roles as a creative technologist, integrating digital planning with physical builds.
My technical capabilities include woodworking, drilling, and precision assembly, alongside the operation of 3D printers and laser cutters. I utilize 2D and 3D design software to plan the structural integrity of projects before moving into the physical fabrication phase. The projects showcased here illustrate my ability to prototype functional hardware and custom enclosures for interactive installations.
