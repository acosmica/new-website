I spent a year designing and conducting 14-week intensive courses for the Humanities Department: "XR and Interactive Media" and "Principles of Multimedia and Web Design." My pedagogy balances rigorous theoretical debate with real-world simulation through software tutorials and hands-on assignments. In the XR lab, we focused on building immersive environments in Unreal Engine using techniques such as 3D scanning. In the web studio, students moved from design theory to functional prototyping with Figma, preparing them for the multidisciplinary demands of the creative industry.
