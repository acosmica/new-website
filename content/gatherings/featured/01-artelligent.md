We are surrounded by artificial intelligences. They learn with us, decide with us, and create with us. ARTELLIGENT emerges as a response to this contemporary phenomenon — Brazil's first festival entirely dedicated to the intersection of art and artificial intelligence. A space to imagine, question, and experiment with what happens when algorithms become present, in the most varied ways, within artistic production.

More than just an event, ARTELLIGENT is a platform for convergence among art, science, and education — bringing together national and international artists, scientists, philosophers, educators, thinkers, and the general public to celebrate creation and discuss the ethical, aesthetic, and social trajectories of this new frontier. Envisioning the future is a collective act.

In its first edition, ARTELLIGENT secured investments from CAIXA Cultural and took place in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil from August to November 2025. The program featured workshops, roundtables, lectures, and a 90-day gallery exhibition.

As Co-Founder and Lead Producer, I was instrumental in the foundational vision and operational architecture of ARTELLIGENT. My role involves a dual focus: supporting the high-level creative direction and meticulous project management. I oversee the curatorial selection of artists and technologists — both assisting the curators and in direct contact with creators — and I work in the area between the conceptual goals of the festival and the logistical realities of execution, from securing partnerships to managing the production of complex technical installations.
