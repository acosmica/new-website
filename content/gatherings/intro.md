My practice has always existed at the intersection of making and sharing. Beyond my personal work as a creative technologist and artist, I have dedicated my career to building the infrastructures where technology, education and culture can meet — as a lead producer for international festivals, as an educator in the classroom, and as a researcher in academia. The focus is always the same: democratization, debate, and questioning.

From leading large-scale immersive festivals to hosting free community workshops, these gatherings are where the magic truly happens — in the space between the interface and the person. Below you'll find the archives of the events I've produced, the communities I've helped build, and the educational spaces where I continue to learn alongside my students.
