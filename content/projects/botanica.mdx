## Overview

<Figure src="/projects/botanica/assets/botanica-03.png" aspect="16/9" />

BOTANICA transforms the immersive dome into a living, responsive sanctuary where the boundaries between organic life and digital code blur. Inspired by the inherent fluidity of nature, the piece features a computer-generated ecosystem that reacts in real-time to the presence of the audience. As visitors move through the space, depth cameras and sensors track their gestures, allowing them to physically influence the swaying of flowers and the unfolding of petals. This interaction creates a dialogue between human movement and machine-generated growth, turning the spectator into a participant in the garden's evolution.

## Process

<ImageAside src="/projects/botanica/assets/botanica-01.png" aspect="9/16" side="left">
The piece explores the concept of "speculative botany", using generative AI to dream up species that do not exist yet in our physical world. Within the media server, custom algorithms work alongside AI models to synthesize new forms inspired by existing flowers, focusing on the inventive potential of technology to imagine new possible existences and bring dreams to physicality. As an interactive designer for thecode, I was responsible for the visual storytelling and for the AI fine-tuning, feeding the system with botanical references and adjusting the prompt for best outputs. The result is a space of co-creation where visitors meet hardware and nature simultaneously.
