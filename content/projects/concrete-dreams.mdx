## Overview

Concrete Dreams is a poetic reimagining of the built environment and the structures we choose to immortalize. In this experience, the solid, immobile permanence of concrete is juxtaposed with the ephemeral fluidity of human motion. By transforming the spectator into a co-creator, the work investigates how monuments relate to society and the ways in which our collective identity can reshape the history written in stone. It serves as an exploration of the interconnectedness of our bodies and our cities, suggesting that even the most rigid structures are subject to the influence of those who move amongst them. What we choose to immortalize?

## Process

The piece is an artistic collaboration with artist Francisco Barretto. Powered by a real-time AI video generation system created specifically for this piece implementing the MediaPipe ML framework and using a RealSense depth-camera that interprets body movement and translates into visual transformation. As visitors interact with the installation, their movements act as metaphors, directly shaping the form and evolution of AI-imagined monuments. This creates a feedback loop where human gestures dictate the structural integrity and aesthetic of the digital environment.
