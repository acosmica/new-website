## Overview

The Chill Throne was designed to be the ultimate companion for the modern sports fan, combining the portability of a folding chair with the high-end amenities of a home lounge. As a Creative Technologist at Smooth Technology, I had the chance to contribute to bringing this experiential project to life, being involved in R&D specifically focused in Physical Computing and the final fabrication and assembly of electronic components. Produced in an ultra-limited run of only ten units, the project garnered significant cultural attention.

<Figure src="/projects/coors-light/assets/coors-light-01.png" aspect="16/9" />

## Process

This brand activation focused on elevating the "chill" experience, transforming a standard piece of outdoor equipment into a multi-sensory hub of relaxation and convenience tailored specifically for the brand's identity. The throne is packed with a suite of custom-engineered amenities, including a seat warmer, back massager, integrated Bluetooth speakers and more. I worked closely on the fabrication and assembly of most of the electronics features, testing each element and finding the best balance between function and aesthetics.
