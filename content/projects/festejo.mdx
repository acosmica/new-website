## Overview

<Figure src="/projects/festejo/assets/festejo-01.png" aspect="16/9" />

This work serves as a kaleidoscopic exploration of the diversity and multiplicity of my roots in Maranhão. By mixing 2D and 3D elements with a vivid color palette, the projection pays homage to the aesthetics of São João festivities and the historical architecture of the region.

<Pair a="/projects/festejo/assets/festejo-02.png" b="/projects/festejo/assets/festejo-03.png" aspect="16/9" />

The narrative is driven by the poetics of encounter, where the physical weight of sequins and ribbons meets the weightless fluidity of pixels. It is a visual celebration of folklore tales passed down through generations, captured as if seen through the wide-eyed, inventive imagination of a child wandering through a festive, historic cityscape.

## Process

<Figure src="/projects/festejo/assets/festejo-04.png" aspect="16/9" />

The piece is a hybrid experiment in texture and medium, intentionally blurring the lines between the handmade and the machine-generated. Hand-drawn illustrations created in Procreate provide a human, tactile foundation, which is then layered with imagery synthesized through artificial intelligence.

To further emphasize the tension between tradition and technology, real-world footage of cultural manifestations is subjected to digital distortion and glitch effects. These digital manipulations act as a metaphor for the evolution of memory, suggesting that while traditions are inherited, they are also constantly reshaped, reimagined and refracted by the modern tools we use to archive them.

The final piece was created specifically for this architectural facade, turning the building into a live storytelling vessel for this magic history.
