## Overview

Created for the VaideBet brand activation at the SoulJoão music festival in Paraíba, Kaleidoscope is a spatial experience that places the participant at the center of a digital mirror world. The brand asked for an innovative "instagrammable" space at their event, where visitors would feel compelled to take pictures for social media.

<Figure src="/projects/kaleidoscope/assets/kaleidoscope-04.png" aspect="16/9" />

The installation features a geometric tunnel with mirrored walls designed to infinitely amplify a large-scale LED panel, creating an environment of constant visual flux. As festival-goers move through the space, the installation acts as a responsive instrument, reflecting their presence through a shifting landscape of colors and shapes.

<Figure src="/projects/kaleidoscope/assets/kaleidoscope-03.png" aspect="9/4" />

## Process

<ImageAside src="/projects/kaleidoscope/assets/kaleidoscope-01.jpg" aspect="2/3" side="left">
The installation's core is powered by OpenFrameworks, utilizing depth cameras to detect and track body positions in real time. This spatial data is translated into generative visuals that respond with precision to every gesture. The mirror tunnel serves to break down the boundaries of the physical structure, creating an "infinity effect", so the visitors feel engulfed by the lights. The use of custom-coded geometric patterns ensures a sharp, vibrant aesthetic that matches the high intensity of the festival environment, turning the participant's silhouette into the primary architect of the tunnel's visual reality.

As the Creative Director for thecode, I pitched the idea and oversaw the project from initial conceptualization to final structural assembly.
