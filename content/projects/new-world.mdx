## Overview

For "The New World Tour," Smooth Technology was invited to complement the stage architecture with their cutting-edge technology. The challenge was focused on high-tech aesthetic and included wearables and a radio-controlled light system. As a Creative Technologist working at the studio, I had the chance to collaborate on building this expansive LED display spanning the stage truss, which was designed to create a unified visual ecosystem. The result was a responsive performance environment that translated the energy of the music into a precise, technological spectacle, moving with the performer.

<Figure src="/projects/world-tour/assets/childish-01.jpg" aspect="4/3" />

## Process

This effort was a collective dedication of many teams. While working at Smooth Technology, I participated in the final stages of prototyping the LED display electronics and the steps of assembling, wiring and soldering the LED individual pieces for shipping.
