## Overview

<Figure src="/projects/symphony/assets/NIME_02.png" aspect="16/9" />

Symphony of a Ritual serves as a live investigation into how historical concepts of sacred space and collective memory translate into modern interaction design. This performance-based work creates a hybrid environment where the "witch" and the "technologist" meet, transforming the stage into a digital altar.

<Pair a="/projects/symphony/assets/NIME_03.png" b="/projects/symphony/assets/NIME_05.png" aspect="16/9" />

By treating technology as a ritualistic medium, the piece invites the audience to witness the manifestation of sound and light as a ritual of co-creation. Using creative coding frameworks, the artist's physical gestures and ritualistic movements are captured and translated into expanding audio and visual content that echo the themes of ancestry, magic and the archive.

## Process

The performance integrates a sophisticated system that, through computer vision, detects the movements of objects in its array of sensors and translates into audio and video signals, generating a spatial experience in real time.

<Figure src="/projects/symphony/assets/NIME_04.png" aspect="16/9" />

The sound and visuals are created in real time in an improvisational lens, making each performance unique in a co-creation with the audience. The use of distorted, layered soundscapes creates a visceral sensory vibration of the room calling the audience into embodiment.
