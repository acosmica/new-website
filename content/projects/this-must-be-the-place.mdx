## Overview

This installation investigates "home" as an elusive, multi-faceted concept that often defies verbal description. Set within a large room filled with scattered fabric curtains, the work functions as a four-minute non-linear journey through the intricate interplay between dreaming and dwelling. By projecting sliced videos and contours onto the ethereal landscape of the hanging fabrics, the piece mirrors the way we seek, find, lose, and rediscover our sense of belonging. The experience invites participants to wander through a narrative shattering that reflects the fragmented nature of our own subconscious associations and memories.

## Process

<Pair a="/projects/this-must-be-the-place/assets/thismust-01.jpeg" b="/projects/this-must-be-the-place/assets/thismust-02.jpeg" aspect="3/4" />

This art piece was created in deep collaboration with multidisciplinary artists Elyana Javaheri, Orpheas Kofinakos, Vinita Gatne, and Anna Nikaki. Our storytelling started with long conversations about interests, our cultural background and our similarities and differences. The process then centered on a collective curation of video fragments and cinematic pieces, which were then carefully edited into a cohesive but non-linear unit. The final result utilizes projection mapping to cast ephemeral illuminations across a multi-layered display made of scattered pieces of fabrics. The experience is heightened by a meticulously composed spatial audio score that evokes the dream-like state, with whispers reciting poetry with verses shaped in collaboration with a machine learning model trained on a vast archive of poetic expressions regarding the essence of home.

<Figure src="/projects/this-must-be-the-place/assets/thismust-04.jpeg" aspect="3/2" />

We wanted to create an immersive space so visitors would need to walk around to appreciate the complete piece, in a physical embodiment of the own concept of seeking home. In that sense, the synthesis of human sentiment, exhibition design and algorithmic word processing created a whimsical, atmospheric environment where the eternal quest for belonging is felt rather than told.
